Alok Kumar Maurya
9 mins

Kohli can never be a great captain although he is a great batsman. Many of you remember that in CT final, first he chose to bowl first and second he didn't give over to Kedar Jadhav before 40 overs who was last match hero against bangladesh. If you remember south africa 2nd test in which bhuvneswar was dropped who was excellent with the ball and batted well too in 1st test.

now you are watching IPL.......against CSK
He selected grandhome in place of Chris Woakes (ok) ..oh ...he didn't give ball to him and Anderson is his death bowler(really). Instead of selecting Anderson he could select Tim southee who is good bowler. And in place of pawan negi an extra batsman could be played like Pawan Deshpande/ Manan Vohra/Macullam/Moeen Ali. Still they would have six bowling option.

If you give this RCB squad to MS dhoni, he will give back to back win to RCB because RCB squad have strength but Virat playing 11 is not strong and his captaincy skill (specialy bowling).

Some people say under his(Kohli) captaincy India won many series, in overseas too. But Where captaincy matter? Captaincy doesn't matter when you have very strong playing XI as compare to the opposition. Captaincy matters where you have marginal chance to win and you won with your captaincy effort(Choosing playing XI, using bowler wisely, batting order setup).

I wish he will smash all the world batting record in future. But he need to work on his Captaincy

Note - I am fan of both.......Virat kohli as a great Batsman ( but not as a Captain) and MS Dhoni as a Great captain and finisher(but sometimes I don't like his tuk-tuk in initial balls ).

I don't like blind follower of both.
